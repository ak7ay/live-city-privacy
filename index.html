Privacy Policy for Live Bengaluru

Effective date: April 9, 2026

Live Bengaluru (the "App") is developed and maintained by Hanif. This policy explains what information the App handles and how it is used.

Information we collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There is no user account, no login, and no in-app analytics or advertising.

Network requests

To display metal prices, news articles, and events, the App makes read-only network requests to a backend hosted on Appwrite Cloud (Singapore region) and loads images from a public content delivery network (CDN). As part of standard server operation, those services may log technical information such as your IP address, device type, and request timestamps. This information is handled by the respective service providers under their own privacy policies.

Permissions

The App requests only the INTERNET permission, which is required to fetch content from the backend. No other permissions are requested.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date above will always reflect the most recent version.
